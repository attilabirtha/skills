# ProClick Offer Elements

Use this reference when an offer needs more detail than the default five-page structure.

## Pricing

- Show VAT-included prices as primary values.
- Show net prices below the VAT-included price in smaller muted text.
- Keep all totals consistent across cover, budget narrative, media table, and service fee.
- If the total budget is VAT-included, calculate media budget after subtracting the VAT-included service fee.
- Use exact net values when needed, e.g. `20.411,76 lei + TVA`; use rounded VAT-included values for commercial readability.

## Service Modules

Use six service cards by default. Expand wording only when the user asks for more detail.

- Strategie de lansare: positioning, funnel, segmentation, 90-day calendar, message directions, product prioritization.
- Audit tracking: GA4, GTM, Meta Pixel, Shopify events, Google Ads conversions, UTM structure, data quality.
- Administrare PPC: Google Search/Shopping/PMax, Meta prospecting and remarketing, catalog ads, creative testing, sales optimization.
- Optimizare conversii: UX audit for homepage, collections, product pages, cart, checkout, and CRO priorities.
- Optimizare feed + CSS: product titles, categories, attributes, Shopping feed, CSS Partner benefit up to 20% discount.
- Raportare consolidata: dashboard and monthly reporting for spend, revenue, ROAS, POAS, CAC, AOV, winning products.

## Strategy Blocks

Three-stage launch page:

- Pregatire: tracking, Shopify, feed, KPI, product priorities, first creative directions.
- Lansare: Google for demand capture; Meta for demand generation, social proof, catalog ads, remarketing.
- Optimizare: move budget toward channels, audiences, creatives, and products that are sellable for promotion and sales.

Use this line when expectations need calibration:

`Pentru [CLIENT], prima luna trebuie sa valideze trackingul, mesajele si produsele vandabile pentru promovare si vanzare. Abia dupa aceea crestem presiunea media.`

## Media Allocation

Default eCommerce launch mix when social is expected to matter more:

- Google Ads: 40%
- Meta Ads: 60%

Optional channels only when requested or justified by content and budget:

- TikTok Ads: for short-form video hook tests and new audiences.
- YouTube Ads: for video reach, remarketing, and search lift.
- Pinterest Ads: for decor, lifestyle, gifting, interior, and inspiration-led categories.

Do not include TikTok, YouTube, or Pinterest automatically if the user asks to keep services and budgets focused on Google and Meta.

## PPC KPI Set

Keep KPIs PPC-specific unless the user asks for broader business metrics:

- Vizibilitate: spend, reach, impressions, CPM, frequency.
- Trafic: CTR, CPC, clicks, landing page views, traffic quality.
- Conversii: add-to-cart, checkout initiated, purchase, cost per purchase, conversion rate.
- Eficienta: ROAS, conversion value, cost per acquisition, budget moved to winning campaigns.

## Reporting

Default reporting cadence:

- Weekly short report: budget evolution, conclusions, actions performed.
- Monthly extended report: results, content recommendations, Shopify/CRO recommendations, next-month plan.
- Biweekly alignment meeting: commercial decisions, channel priorities, creative test validation.

## Validation Checklist

Before delivering:

- Render each affected PDF page to PNG.
- Check no text is clipped.
- Check logo contrast on every page.
- Check service fee, media budget, and total budget reconcile.
- Check channels in services, strategy, and media table match each other.
- Check net and VAT-included prices are not visually confused.
